Before understanding the working of Szilards Engine, we must ensure that we are acquainted with what a Qubit is ! A qubit is the smallest fundamental unit of a Quantum System and is analogous to the classical bit of information which can either be 0 or 1. But a qubit is a piece of information which stores the information in an entangled and superpo- sition state such that it can be both 0 and 1 or anything in between but is only realised when it is measured. It is represented as follows:- | = |0 + |1 On measuring the qubit, it collapses to either |0 or |1. Now, A Szilards Engine uses a Four-Qubit System (Each Qubit is a Two Level System). Qubit W: Weight Qubit P: Particles Position Qubit M: Demons Memory Qubit A: To Reset The Information Thus, Szilards Engine uses a single particle which is trapped within a box surrounded by a thermal reservoir. A demon like entity places a partition in between the box in order to identify and remember the position of the particle whether left or right side. The particle extracts heat from the thermal reservoir to push the partition and lift the weight which is placed by the demon based on the memory of where the particle is. The particle collides with the partition and all the extracted heat energy is converted into work. Now it might seem the complete conversion of heat into work is being done which is a certain violation of the Second Law Of Thermodynamics. But here comes the Landauers Principle to the rescue. Landauers Principle states that the process of creating information requires W>k b Tln(2) of energy and process of erasing a bit of information releases Q<k B Tln(2) of heat energy. Now we should realise that the measuring apparatus(or the Maxwells Demon) still has the memory or information of the particles position. Thus to complete the cycle we must reset the Demons Memory. This way Szilards Engine is a vital example which introduces the signicance of Information Thermodynamics. 5
